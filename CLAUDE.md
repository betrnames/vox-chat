# Vox.chat — AI Automation Consulting for Local Service Businesses

## Brand
- Domain: vox.chat
- Tagline: "Your business. Never offline."
- Target: solo operators and small service contractors (5-15 techs) in Central Valley CA
- Tone: premium, direct, zero fluff

## Design System
- **Background:** #0A0A0F (near-black)
- **Primary accent:** #00D4AA (green)
- **Voice service:** #FF6B4A (warm red-orange)
- **Chat service:** #4A9EFF (blue)
- **Review service:** #FFB84A (amber)
- **Fonts:** Space Grotesk (headings), DM Sans (body), Space Mono (monospace/labels)
- **Style:** Dark-first, mobile-first, luxury minimalist (Linear/Vercel/Stripe aesthetic)
- **Anti-slop:** NO Inter, NO Roboto, NO purple gradients, NO generic SaaS layouts

## Three Services

### Vox Voice — AI Phone Agent ($800–$1,500/mo)
- AI answers business phone 24/7
- Qualifies caller, captures name/phone/address/issue
- Books appointment to contractor's calendar
- Sends instant text/email notification to contractor
- Call recordings and transcripts
- Custom script per business
- Color: #FF6B4A

### Vox Chat — Website Chatbot ($500–$800/mo)
- Smart chat widget on contractor's website
- Custom-trained on their services and pricing
- Captures lead info: name, phone, service needed
- Answers FAQs automatically
- Schedules appointments
- Leads sent to email or CRM
- Color: #4A9EFF

### Vox Reviews — Review Automation ($300–$500/mo)
- Automated text sent 2 hours after job completion
- Follow-up text at 48 hours if no response
- Direct link to Google Business Profile review page
- Negative review alert sent to owner first
- Monthly review growth report
- Color: #FFB84A

### Bundle: All three for $1,500/mo

## Required Sections
1. Nav (logo + CTA)

5. ROI stats ($2,500 lost/week, 312% ROI month one, 4.8 star avg)

- React + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS (Vite)
- Deploy: Vercel
- Form: Formspree (free tier)
- Demos: client-side, hardcoded responses
