## Price vs alternatives (anchors)

| Alternative | Rough cost | Compared to Vox Bundle $1,500 |
|-------------|------------|-------------------------------|
| Part-time receptionist | ~$1,500–$1,800/mo | Similar $ · only partial hours |
| Full-time front desk | ~$2,750–$3,500/mo + benefits | More expensive · still not 24/7 |
| Answering service only | ~$500–$1,500/mo | Often cheaper · messages, not full stack |
| DIY AI tools | ~$20–$150/mo | Much cheaper · you do the work |
| Smith.ai-class (real volume) | Often $300–$2,000+ | Overlaps; add-ons/overages common |

**Break-even story:**  
One recovered $500–$1,000 job often covers a big chunk of monthly price.  
Conservative: **2–3 booked jobs/mo** from missed calls pays for Voice; **1 solid job** often covers Reviews.

## Where the money goes (internal)

There is **no revenue split** with Twilio, xAI, or Vapi. Vendors bill **you**. Client bills **you** only.

```
Client pays (example Bundle)   $1,500
Tools (typical moderate use)   ~$40–$250
Card fee (~3% if Stripe)       ~$45
────────────────────────────────────
Most of the rest is yours      (before tax and your time)
```

### Rough tool costs (update from real bills)

| Tool | Role | Ballpark |
|------|------|----------|
| Grok API | Chat + voice brain | Often a few $ to low tens $/mo per client |
| Twilio SMS | Reviews + alerts | ~$0.008/text · often under ~$5–15 for a small crew |
| Vapi + phone minutes | Voice (when live) | Often ~$0.15–0.35/min all-in |
| Vercel / Formspree / Google | Hosting, forms, sheet | $0–low |

### Illustrative margin by package

| Package | Client pays | Tools (light–moderate) | Roughly yours* |
|---------|-------------|------------------------|----------------|
| Reviews $400 | $400 | ~$5–25 | ~$375–395 |
| Receptionist $650 | $650 | ~$10–50 | ~$600–640 |
| Voice $1,100 | $1,100 | ~$25–200 | ~$900–1,075 |
| Bundle $1,500 | $1,500 | ~$40–250 | ~$1,250–1,460 |

\*Before tax and your labor. Heavy call volume raises Voice tool cost — then raise list or define fair use; don’t add surprise fees.

## Close checklist

1. Agree package + **list price** out loud  
2. **Collect first month** (Stripe / Zelle — no Net)  
3. Send setup form: `https://vox.chat/setup?source=...&interest=...`  
4. Configure **only** what they paid for  
5. Go live · check in day 7–14
