## 1. Do we target 3 local cities? (Yes — and Turlock is #1)

**Yes.** Three cities is the right side-hustle wedge: small enough to own, large enough for 8–15 clients, tight enough for coffee / BNI / same-day onsite.

### Recommended triangle (from Turlock)

| Priority | City | Why |
|----------|------|-----|
| **1 — Home base** | **Turlock** | You live here. Trust, BNI, face-to-face, “local guy.” First case studies should say *Turlock*. |
| **2 — Density** | **Modesto** | Bigger contractor market, ~15–20 min. Same media market, same 209 habits. |
| **3 — Proof + content** | **Manteca** | You already have SEO/blog capital here. Strong HVAC demand corridor toward Tracy/Lathrop. Still driveable for a kickoff meeting. |

**Why not more than 3 yet:** every extra city dilutes BNI, Google, and word-of-mouth. Own this triangle until you have **5+ paying clients** and **3 case studies** with city + trade in the title.

**Why not Merced/Stockton first:** fine later. Merced is smaller and further; Stockton is larger but stretches your “I show up” brand. Expand *after* the triangle works.

### How to use the triangle in every conversation

> “I work with HVAC, plumbing, and electrical companies in **Turlock, Modesto, and Manteca** — AI that runs phones, customer conversations, and reviews so they stop losing jobs after hours.”

One geography. One ICP. One stack.

### Local brand line (memorize)

> **Vox.chat — AI front desk for Central Valley service businesses.**  
> Based in Turlock. Built for crews who can’t sit by the phone.

## 5. Missed Call Audit (productized free consult)

**Name:** Missed Call Audit  
**Length:** 15 minutes  
**Price:** Free  
**Goal:** One clear number + one next step (Reviews or Bundle). Not a free custom project.

### Before the call (2 minutes of prep)

Ask them to text or email (or estimate on the call):

1. Rough **calls per day** (peak season if seasonal)  
2. Do they answer **after 5pm / weekends**?  
3. Approx **Google review count + rating**  
4. Average job ticket (or “mostly service / mostly install”)

### Audit script (minute by minute)

**0–2 · Frame**  
“This is a quick audit, not a software demo. We’ll estimate what unanswered calls and weak reviews might be costing you in Turlock / Modesto / Manteca, then I’ll tell you if Vox is even a fit.”

**2–6 · Questions**  
1. Who answers the phone during jobs? After hours?  
2. What happens when they miss a call — voicemail? Callback when?  
3. How many jobs per week feel like they came from Google / “who answered first”?  
4. Who asks for reviews today? How often?  
5. Ever hired an answering service or receptionist?

**6–10 · Math (use their numbers; defaults if they guess)**  

| Input | Default if unknown |
|-------|---------------------|
| Missed or delayed calls / week | 5 |
| Close rate if they had answered | 40–50% |
| Avg ticket | $400–$600 service |

**Simple formula**  
`Missed/week × 4 × close% × avg ticket ≈ monthly leak`

Example:  
`5 × 4 × 0.45 × $500 ≈ $4,500/mo` walking out the door.

**Reviews (30 seconds)**  
“Under ~50 reviews in a competitive map pack city, you’re often invisible vs. the guy with 100+. Automation is how small crews catch up without begging at the door.”

**10–13 · Fit check**  
- Fit: local service, phone-driven, 209 triangle, owner feels the pain  
- Not a fit: pure commercial bid-only, no phone demand, wants custom app build  

**13–15 · Close**  
One path only:

> “Based on this, I’d start you on **[Reviews / Voice / Bundle]** at **[price]**. Setup is one short call; month-to-month. Want to lock a setup slot this week?”

If not ready:  
> “No problem. I’ll send a one-pager. When a call gets missed this weekend, you’ll know the number we just ran.”

- 2nd–3rd client

- BNI weekly ask stays specific  
- Goal: **$1.5k–$3k MRR**

### Days 61–90 — Rhythm  
- Default close = Bundle for anyone with real after-hours pain  
- Monthly report template live  
- 3 written testimonials with **city + trade**  
- Goal: **3–5 clients**, decide if BNI room is producing intros
